Our Story

About Lighthouse Landscape

Founded in 1998, Lighthouse Landscape is a family-owned business dedicated to enhancing outdoor spaces throughout Long Beach Island. Our team of skilled professionals brings expertise in landscape design, maintenance, and installation, with special attention to the unique coastal environment of LBI.

We understand the local soil conditions, weather patterns, and plant varieties that thrive in this beautiful shore community.

Local Expertise

Deep understanding of coastal plantings and maintenance requirements specific to LBI.

Property Care

Custom maintenance programs for both year-round and seasonal homes.

Quality Assurance

Premium materials and meticulous workmanship for lasting beauty.
